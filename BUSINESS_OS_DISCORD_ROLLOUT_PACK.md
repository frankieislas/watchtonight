# Business OS - Discord Rollout Pack

Use the following messages to initialize the Discord server as a business operating system.

---

## Post in #command-desk
We are now treating this Discord as a business operating system, not just a chat space.

This channel is the command desk.
Use it for:
- top priorities
- approvals
- key decisions
- blockers that affect real progress
- final calls on experiments and allocation

If something materially affects users, money, top priorities, or weekly bets, it should become visible here.

Current goal:
Turn this system into a tighter execution and decision engine that gets to users, signups, and revenue faster.

---

## Post in #scoreboard
This channel is now the source of truth for:
- active projects
- top bets
- growth signals
- revenue signals
- experiment status
- blockers

The rule:
If it matters to business performance, it should roll up here.

---

## Post in #weekly-review
This channel is for weekly review only.

Every week we will answer:
- what moved demand?
- what moved revenue odds?
- what was noise?
- what should we keep, kill, or pivot?

The goal is not activity. The goal is clarity and better decisions.

---

## Post in each role channel
This channel exists to produce outputs, not just discussion.

Use it for the work owned by this role.
Important outcomes should roll up into command-desk and scoreboard when they affect direction.

---

## Post in #blockers
Use this channel for blockers that need:
- a decision
- help from another role
- priority escalation
- infrastructure or system intervention

Do not let important blockers disappear into chat.

---

## Post in #wins
Use this channel to capture meaningful wins worth reinforcing.
Not vanity. Only things that improved users, signups, revenue odds, or system quality.
