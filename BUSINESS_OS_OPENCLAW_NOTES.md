# Business OS - OpenClaw Notes

## How OpenClaw should fit
OpenClaw should act as the execution system under the business OS, not as a separate parallel world.

## Recommended use
- Command-desk decisions happen in Discord
- Role outputs can be generated or advanced through OpenClaw sessions
- Important outputs get rolled back into command-desk and scoreboard

## Main rule
Do not let work disappear into isolated sessions without summaries, decisions, or artifacts.

## Best behavior
- short loop from work to artifact to decision
- fewer vague updates, more concrete outputs
- strong distinction between execution lanes and command layer
