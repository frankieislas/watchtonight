# Business OS - Channel Starter Messages

## #scout
Scout owns opportunities, user pain, target segments, and market signals.

Expected outputs:
- viable opportunities
- demand signals
- user segment ideas
- what looks worth testing next

## #builder
Builder owns product execution, shipping, bugs, implementation, and instrumentation.

Expected outputs:
- what shipped
- where it lives
- what changed
- blockers needing decisions

## #closer
Closer owns offers, pricing, conversion, monetization tests, and getting closer to first revenue.

Expected outputs:
- premium hooks
- pricing hypotheses
- conversion ideas
- money-linked recommendations

## #analyst
Analyst owns metrics, experiment readouts, scoreboard updates, and distinguishing signal from noise.

Expected outputs:
- scoreboards
- readouts
- experiment summaries
- recommendations on what to scale or cut

## #operator
Operator owns process, workflows, automations, systems, and reliability.

Expected outputs:
- workflow improvements
- recurring systems
- automation opportunities
- reduced friction

## #archivist
Archivist owns docs, memory, lessons, and reusable playbooks.

Expected outputs:
- updated docs
- preserved learnings
- decision history
- clean operating memory
