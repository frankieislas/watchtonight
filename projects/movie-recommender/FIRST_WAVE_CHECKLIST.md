# First Wave Checklist

## Before sharing
- hosted page works
- form works
- thank-you page works
- copy feels good enough
- link is ready

## During first wave
- share to 5 to 15 trusted people
- use one simple message
- ask one simple core question
- avoid over-explaining

## After first wave
- count actual signups
- review repeated comments
- identify the strongest positive signal
- identify the clearest friction point
- decide whether to tighten copy or move toward a slightly broader launch
