# WatchTonight 7-Day Beta Sprint

## Day 1
- send link to first 10 testers
- gather first reactions

## Day 2
- send to 10 more testers
- track repeated complaints and best reactions

## Day 3
- tighten biggest product issues only
- ask best testers if they would use it again

## Day 4
- send to couples / date-night testers specifically
- compare reactions with solo users

## Day 5
- test lightweight pricing language
- ask what premium value would be worth paying for

## Day 6
- identify strongest segment
- identify strongest recurring value prop

## Day 7
- decide whether to double down on consumer premium, concierge, or another monetization path
