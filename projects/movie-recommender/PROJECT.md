# Movie Recommender Project

## Working name
WatchTonight

## Core concept
A personalized streaming concierge that helps people decide what movie to watch tonight in under a minute.

## Simple value proposition
Stop scrolling. Get 3 great movies to watch tonight.

## Problem
People with multiple streaming subscriptions waste time browsing and still struggle to pick something they actually want to watch.

## Proposed solution
Recommend the top 3 movies to watch tonight based on:
- the streaming services the user already has
- current discussion and trend signals
- quality signals and reputation
- the user's accumulated taste profile

## Target customer
Primary:
- people with multiple streaming subscriptions
- people who waste time browsing before choosing a movie
- people who want a fast, trusted answer instead of a giant catalog

Best early segment:
- busy professionals
- couples trying to decide what to watch together
- casual movie watchers with too many subscriptions

## MVP promise
A lightweight recommendation product that gives users the top 3 movies to watch tonight based on the streaming services they already have, what is trending, quality signals, and their personal taste profile, helping them make a confident decision in under a minute instead of wasting time browsing.

## MVP features
Must-have:
- choose streaming services
- store taste preferences
- top 3 movie picks
- where to watch
- why each pick fits
- one best pick if the user only watches one
- feedback loop from watched / rated movies

Not needed yet:
- mobile app
- advanced social features
- deep community features
- heavy account system
- large editorial catalog

## Delivery options
Near-term MVP delivery could be:
- Discord
- email
- simple web app

Recommended first build path:
- validate with landing page + waitlist first
- then build a simple web MVP

## Business model hypothesis
Most likely early model:
- free waitlist
- free beta
- later premium personalization

Possible pricing later:
- $5 to $10 per month
- potentially low-cost annual plan

## Product positioning
This is not a review site and not a giant catalog browser.
It is a decision product that gives fast, personal, high-signal movie picks.
