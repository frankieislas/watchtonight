# Soft Launch Prep

## Short product description
WatchTonight is a personalized streaming recommendation product that helps people decide what movie to watch tonight without wasting time scrolling. It filters recommendations by the streaming services you already use, your taste, and what is worth watching right now.

## Recommendation: what soft launch should mean
Soft launch should mean:
- share only with a small number of trusted people first
- do not post broadly or publicly yet
- use it to gather early reactions, not scale traffic
- watch for confusion, friction, and whether people actually sign up without hand-holding
- keep positioning flexible based on what early users respond to

## Recommended soft-launch shape in practice
- share with 5 to 15 people first
- prefer friends, trusted contacts, and people likely to give honest reactions
- ask a simple question along with the link: "Is this useful enough that you'd sign up or share it?"
- treat the first wave as signal collection, not a real launch campaign

## Final private QA checklist
- page loads cleanly on desktop
- page loads cleanly on mobile
- both CTA forms submit successfully
- custom thank-you page works
- copy feels tight and non-repetitive
- no obviously broken sections or confusing wording
- link is ready for small-scale soft sharing when approved
