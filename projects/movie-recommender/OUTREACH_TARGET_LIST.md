# WatchTonight Outreach Target List

## Goal
Get 25 to 50 real testers quickly, then identify who reuses the app and who might pay.

## Priority groups
### Tier 1 - people most likely to try fast
- friends who regularly watch movies or TV
- couples who complain about wasting time choosing what to watch
- group-chat friends who do movie night
- people who already like Letterboxd / movie recommendations

### Tier 2 - higher-signal testers
- people with multiple streaming subscriptions
- people who like sharing recommendations
- people who are opinionated about movies and UX

### Tier 3 - broader distribution experiments
- Reddit communities
- Discord communities
- group chats with movie lovers
- short-form social posts with the link

## Ask from each tester
- try the app once
- tell us if the recommendations felt accurate
- tell us if it helped them decide faster
- tell us if they would use it again
- tell us what would make it worth paying for
