# Sample Recommendation Experience

## Purpose
Show what WatchTonight actually feels like when it gives a user the top 3 movie picks for the evening.

## Sample output

### Tonight's Top 3 Movie Picks

#### 1. Ex Machina
- Where to watch: Max
- Why it made the list: High-quality sci-fi that is still widely recommended when people want something smart, tense, and visually clean.
- Quality signal: Strong critical reputation and lasting word-of-mouth.
- Vibe: Thoughtful, tense, sleek.
- Taste fit: Great for someone who likes intelligent sci-fi, atmosphere, and contained tension.

#### 2. Palm Springs
- Where to watch: Hulu
- Why it made the list: Frequently recommended as an easy, clever, high-upside watch when someone wants something fun without feeling disposable.
- Quality signal: Strong audience response and consistent recommendation value.
- Vibe: Funny, romantic, light sci-fi.
- Taste fit: Great if the user wants something more playful, rewatchable, and low-friction.

#### 3. Michael Clayton
- Where to watch: Prime Video
- Why it made the list: Still one of the strongest adult dramas people bring up when they want something sharp, serious, and worth the time.
- Quality signal: Excellent critical reputation and durable respect over time.
- Vibe: Smart, serious, gripping.
- Taste fit: Great if the user likes tension, dialogue, and mature storytelling over spectacle.

### If you only watch one tonight
Ex Machina, because it best combines quality, accessibility, strong identity, and easy recommendation confidence.
