# WatchTonight Beta Metrics Scorecard

## Track manually for first beta
- number of testers sent the link
- number who actually opened it
- number who completed the flow
- number who said recommendations were good
- number who said it saved them time
- number who would use it again
- number who expressed willingness to pay

## Strong signs
- users come back on their own
- users say it helped them decide much faster
- users send it to friends
- users ask for more personalization

## Weak signs
- people think it is neat but do not reuse it
- recommendations are only passable, not impressive
- nobody shows willingness to pay
