# Role Ownership Map

## Scout
### Owns
- new opportunities
- target user segments
- market signals
- competitor and trend scans

### Weekly outputs
- 3 to 5 viable opportunities
- 1 short market brief per top opportunity
- 1 recommendation on what to test next

## Builder
### Owns
- shipping product improvements
- bug fixes
- UX polish
- instrumentation hooks

### Weekly outputs
- shipped changes
- build log
- blockers requiring decisions

## Closer
### Owns
- offers
- pricing tests
- conversion points
- outreach copy tied to money

### Weekly outputs
- 1 monetization hypothesis
- 1 pricing / offer test
- 1 follow-up recommendation based on user responses

## Analyst
### Owns
- metrics
- experiment readouts
- scoreboard updates
- signal vs noise judgment

### Weekly outputs
- scoreboard refresh
- experiment summary
- what to double down on / stop

## Operator
### Owns
- systems
- workflows
- automations
- integrations
- reliability and process

### Weekly outputs
- process improvements
- automation opportunities
- workflow cleanup

## Archivist
### Owns
- docs
- playbooks
- lessons learned
- historical continuity

### Weekly outputs
- updated memory/docs
- cleaned project notes
- distilled lessons from recent experiments
