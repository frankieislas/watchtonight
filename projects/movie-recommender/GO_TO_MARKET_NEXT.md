# WatchTonight Go-To-Market Next

## Immediate actions
1. Send the beta link to 25 to 50 people
2. Use the beta tester message template
3. Collect structured feedback with the beta feedback template
4. Track who actually reuses the product
5. Identify the strongest user segment

## Best likely early user segments
- couples who waste time deciding what to watch
- friend groups doing movie night
- busy people who want a fast answer
- people who enjoy recommendation apps and movie culture

## Main question to answer now
Who gets the most value from WatchTonight, and are they willing to pay for that value?

## Immediate product priorities that support monetization
- better result actions: save, not for me, watched, more like this
- stronger memory and personalization
- cleaner recommendation confidence
- lightweight premium positioning in the product
