# Validation Plan

## Goal
Validate whether people want a personalized streaming recommendation product enough to join a waitlist before a full app exists.

## Hypothesis
People with multiple streaming subscriptions want a simple product that tells them the best 3 movies to watch tonight, personalized to their taste and filtered by services they already have.

## Core messages to test
- Stop scrolling. Get 3 great movies to watch tonight.
- Personalized movie picks based on your streaming subscriptions and your taste.
- What should I watch tonight? Solved.
- A streaming concierge for people with too many options.

## Validation assets to create
- landing page
- waitlist form
- short product blurb
- optional mockup / sample recommendation view

## What success looks like
Early positive signals:
- people understand the value quickly
- people say they have this problem already
- people join a waitlist without needing the full product yet
- people respond positively to personalization + subscription filtering

## Questions to answer
- Which message resonates most?
- Is this more compelling as a consumer app, concierge, or simple daily recommendation product?
- Do people care more about trending picks, personalized picks, or streaming-service filtering?
- Is this strong enough for a paid product later?

## Recommended next steps
1. finalize working name
2. finalize landing page copy
3. build a simple waitlist page
4. collect feedback and signup interest
5. refine the product angle based on demand
