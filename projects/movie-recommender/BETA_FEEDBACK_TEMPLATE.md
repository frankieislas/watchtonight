# WatchTonight Beta Feedback Template

## Ask every tester
1. Did the recommendations feel genuinely useful?
2. Did it help you pick faster than your normal process?
3. Would you use it again?
4. What felt wrong, weak, or annoying?
5. What would make this worth paying for?

## Signals to track
- first-time reaction
- whether they completed the flow
- whether they chose one of the suggested movies
- whether they came back later
- whether they shared it with someone else
- willingness to pay or strong interest
