# Week 1 Top Bets

## Bet 1 - WatchTonight beta push
Hypothesis:
If WatchTonight gets in front of 25 to 50 real testers, we will get enough signal on repeat use, signup demand, and premium interest to decide the next product/commercial step.

Success criteria:
- 25+ real testers
- new waitlist signups
- at least several positive reuse / would-pay signals

## Bet 2 - Business OS adoption
Hypothesis:
If the command-desk, scoreboard, and role lanes become the real operating system this week, decisions will get clearer and work will stop fragmenting across channels and sessions.

Success criteria:
- command-desk used for real priorities
- scoreboard updated at least once this week
- blockers and experiments routed through the OS

## Bet 3 - Premium signal test
Hypothesis:
If users are explicitly asked about premium value, some will reveal what specific improvement they would pay for.

Success criteria:
- collect premium-interest responses
- identify strongest premium hook
- decide first monetization test
